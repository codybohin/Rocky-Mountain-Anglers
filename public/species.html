Lake Trout — The Deep-Water Giant

Lake trout (Salvelinus namaycush) are Utah's ultimate trophy — and Flaming Gorge is one of the best places in America to catch a giant. Fish over 50 pounds have come from the Gorge's deep, cold canyon water.

Utah Home Water
Flaming Gorge Reservoir
Trophy Potential

Identification Tips
✓Light spots on dark grey or green body
✓Deeply forked tail — more forked than other trout
✓No pink lateral stripe (unlike rainbow trout)
✓Worm-like markings (vermiculations) near dorsal fin
✓Large head relative to body size
