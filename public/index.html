The Process

How It Works

A verifiable Rocky Mountain fishing leaderboard built on AI-assisted photo verification and community oversight.

Step 01
📦

Get Certified

Order your Certified Angler Kit ($34.99). We ship a branded RMA sticker, certified ruler, and digital scale. The sticker must appear in every leaderboard photo — it's your proof of membership and how we verify catches.

Step 02
📸

Catch and Document

Place the RMA sticker and ruler in frame and take your photo. AI-assisted verification analyzes the sticker, identifies the species, and cross-checks the measurement. Community members can flag anything suspicious.

Step 03
🏆

Submit and Compete

Pay $5 to enter the monthly leaderboard and prize pot. 60% to 1st, 30% to 2nd, 10% to 3rd. Every catch also logs to your personal fish diary with GPS coordinates for private analytics.
