The Process

How It Works

The first AI-verified fishing leaderboard in the Rocky Mountains. Real fish, real measurements, real prizes.

📦

Get Your Kit

Order your Certified Angler Kit. We ship you a branded RMA sticker, certified ruler, and digital scale. The sticker must appear in every leaderboard photo — it's your proof of membership.

📸

Catch & Document

Land a fish, place the sticker and ruler in frame, take your photo. Our AI verifies the sticker presence, identifies the species, and cross-checks the measurement. Community flags suspicious catches.

🏆

Submit & Compete

Pay $5 to enter the monthly leaderboard and prize pot. 60% goes to 1st place, 35% to 2nd, 10% to 3rd. Every catch logs to your fish diary with GPS coordinates for personal analytics.
